Hello, Welcome to The Cake Shop

We are extraordinarily proud to represent the fantastic products of The Cake Shop. By maintaining the highest standards for quality and freshness of globally sourced ingredients, mixing the unique and proven recipes to the most exacting standards, and artistically decorating our prodents, we are certain to provide a consistent bakery product to our customers everytime. Many of our customers state that the Cake Shop is the best bakery in Central Abeokuta.

2000+ Birthday cakes delivered!

We are an award winning bakery that is a source of joy to Nigeria. We specialize in scratch-made, African-style cakes made with unique flavours and custom, elegant designs. We are proud to have been a source of joy and happiness at over 2000+ birthdays in Central Abeokuta since 1997. We are the best wedding cake bakery in Nigeria/Central Abeokuta. We have been making best tasting birthday cakes since 1997.

Custom/3-D/Carved Cakes or party trays for any occasion!

We are a custom cake bakery that specializes in custom/3-D/carved cakes for any occasion. We can design any cake as long as you can describe it or show a picture. It has never been easier to select the perfect desert for your social function. Order from our superb variety of Mini Desserts.

Since 1997, the Cake Shop has been famous for its sumptuous African pastries, confections, and wedding cakes in Abeokuta Nigeria. Our tortes, cheesecakes, wedding cakes, cupcakes, cookies, tarts and special desserts delight the eye and please the palate! Our uncompromising standards for the highest quality ingredients, exacting measurements in product creation, and artistry in presentation of the delectable dessert ensures your absolute delight and breath taking reaction every time. The most unique creations and best cakes in Nigeria/Central Abeokuta are created in the Cake Shop African Bakery. We encourage you to visit the Cake Shop African Bakery. You will most certainly be glad you did!
